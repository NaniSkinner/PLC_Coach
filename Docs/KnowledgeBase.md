## Category 2: Academic Research Papers (Open Access)

### ERIC Database - Professional Learning Communities

**Search Query Used:** "Professional Learning Communities" in ERIC
**Results:** 1000+ open-access papers

**Top Research Papers for Knowledge Base:**

#### 1. "The Effective Implementation of Professional Learning Communities"

- **Authors:** Benjamin D. Brown, Rolanda S. Horn, Gwendolyn King
- **URL:** https://files.eric.ed.gov/fulltext/EJ1194725.pdf
- **Content:** Implementation frameworks, STEM applications, HRO principles
- **Value:** Strong theoretical grounding for coach responses

#### 2. "Professional Learning Communities and Communities of Practice"

- **URL:** https://files.eric.ed.gov/fulltext/ED504776.pdf
- **Content:** Compares PLC models (DuFour, Hord), knowledge development cycle
- **Value:** Explains HOW PLCs facilitate learning - great for coaching context

#### 3. "A Review of Research on the Impact of PLCs on Teaching Practice and Student Learning"

- **Authors:** Vescio et al. (2008)
- **URL:** https://www.researchgate.net/publication/222686637
- **Content:** Meta-analysis of PLC effectiveness, empirical evidence
- **Value:** Data-driven evidence for coaching recommendations

#### 4. "Professional Learning Communities: A Review of the Literature"

- **Authors:** Stoll et al. (2006)
- **Content:** Comprehensive review, capacity building, sustainability
- **Value:** Long-form deep dive into PLC characteristics

#### 5. "Best Practices Guidebook: Professional Learning Communities"

- **URL:** https://files.eric.ed.gov/fulltext/ED602054.pdf
- **Content:** Practical implementation guide, organizational conditions
- **Value:** Actionable steps for coaches to recommend

**Additional ERIC Papers (50+ more available):**

## Category 5: Coaching Conversation Frameworks

### General Coaching Models (Adaptable to PLCs)

**GROW Model Resources:**

#### 1. Coaching Conversation Examples (Positive Psychology)

- **URL:** https://positivepsychology.com/coaching-conversation-examples/
- **Content:** GROW model framework, active listening techniques
- **Adaptable:** YES - can be adapted to PLC team coaching

#### 2. How to Hold Powerful Coaching Conversations (Thinkific)

- **URL:** https://www.thinkific.com/blog/coaching-conversations/
- **Content:** Question types, collaboration principles, support strategies
- **Key Insight:** 90/10 rule (90% listening, 10% talking)

#### 3. Coaching Conversation Scripts (Life Coach Hub)

- **URL:** https://lifecoachhub.com/coaching-articles/coach-like-a-pro-a-dynamic-coaching-conversation-script-with-samples-and-template-for-empowering-sessions/
- **Content:** Active listening, powerful questioning, session structure
- **Templates:** Session planning templates

#### 4. Coaching Conversations: Empowering Employees (BizLibrary)

- **URL:** https://www.bizlibrary.com/blog/employee-development/coaching-conversations/
- **Content:** Real coaching dialogue example (manager-employee)
- **Value:** Shows facilitative vs. directive approach

**Key Coaching Principles Identified:**

1. **Inquiry-based approach** - Ask questions before prescribing
2. **GROW model** - Goal, Reality, Options, Way Forward
3. **Active listening** - Paraphrase, reflect, clarify
4. **Collaboration** - Two-way conversation, shared problem-solving
5. **Support** - Focus on strengths, normalize challenges

**Recommendation:** Use these frameworks to develop 15-20 mock PLC coaching transcripts, replacing generic scenarios with PLC-specific challenges (CFA analysis, intervention planning, etc.).
